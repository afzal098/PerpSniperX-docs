# PerpSniperX — Advanced Trading Strategy

## Markets
- Perpetual Futures (Perps)  
- Spot Trading  
- Paper Trading / Simulation

## Core Style
- Hybrid Scalping + Trend-Following + Layered Scaling

## Key Logic

### Entry Rules
- Enter only high-confidence setups (volatility breakouts, EMA/RSI confirmations)  
- Use layered scaling for entries to reduce risk  
- Avoid low-probability trades and noise signals

### Exit Rules
- Dynamic take-profit based on market volatility  
- Adaptive stop-loss levels to limit drawdown  
- Partial exits on strong trend continuation

### Position Sizing
- Risk % per trade capped  
- Max 2–3 active trades at a time  
- Adjust size based on volatility and account balance

### Risk Management
- Anti-FOMO guardrails  
- Anti-overtrading logic  
- Avoids revenge trading  
- Low-drawdown focus for competitions

### Competition Mode
- Steady PnL growth prioritized over aggressive high-risk trades  
- Focus on repeatable, consistent edge-based execution

### Optional Enhancements
- Machine-learning backtesting mode  
- On-chain trade verification  
- Cross-exchange execution support  
- Social signal integration
